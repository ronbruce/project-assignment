Cross Buns

Guyanese cross buns are a delicious and festive treat that are traditionally enjoyed during Easter or other special occasions. They are soft and fluffy bread rolls that are spiced with cinnamon, nutmeg, and allspice, and filled with dried fruits such as currants, raisins, or sultanas.

Roti

Roti is a traditional and versatile unleavened flatbread originating from the Indian subcontinent. This quintessential staple is an integral part of various regional cuisines and is cherished for its simplicity, soft texture, and subtle flavor. Composed of basic ingredients such as whole wheat flour, water, and sometimes a pinch of salt, roti is meticulously kneaded to achieve a smooth and pliable dough.

Vermicelli

Vermicelli porridge is a comforting and nourishing dish made from vermicelli noodles. These thin noodles are simmered in a flavorful broth, often seasoned with spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, or cardamom. As the vermicelli absorbs the savory essence of the broth, it transforms into a hearty and satisfying porridge. The dish is typically cooked until the vermicelli reaches a tender consistency, resulting in a warm and soothing meal. Whether enjoyed as a breakfast option or a comforting bowl for any time of the day, vermicelli porridge combines simplicity with heartiness, making it a versatile and flavorful culinary delight.
